Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how E-Netter OÜ ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you visit modbusmanager.com or purchase and use ModbusManager software.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Who We Are

Data Controller:
E-Netter OÜ (operating as ModbusManager)
Email: support@modbusmanager.com

2. What Data We Collect

DataWhen collectedPurpose
Email addressWhen you purchase a licenseSending license key and order confirmation
Machine IDWhen you request license activationGenerating your unique license key

Important: ModbusManager software itself does not collect any data from your computer. It works completely offline and does not send any telemetry, usage statistics or device information to our servers.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment card details (handled by Paddle)
  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties
  • The software does not send any data from your computer to our servers

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — processing your order and delivering your license key
  • Legitimate interests — providing customer support and preventing fraud
  • Legal obligation — retaining financial records as required by law

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Order and license records — 7 years (required by Estonian accounting law)
  • Support emails — 2 years after the last communication
  • Machine ID — for the duration of your license

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@modbusmanager.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss or disclosure. All communication with our website is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

9. Cookies

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are viewed). Google Analytics sets cookies and may process aggregated, largely anonymised usage and device data. We do not use these cookies for advertising or to identify you personally.

You can refuse or delete cookies in your browser settings, or install Google's official opt-out browser add-on to stop Google Analytics on all sites.

10. Children's Privacy

ModbusManager is a professional tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
